Rules

Your goal si easy: reach 100 points to win the game

During your turn, you can decide at any time to:

  • Click on the Roll Dice button: each result will be added to your current score BUT if you roll a 1, your current score is lost and your turn is over (so don't forget to hold once in a while)
  • Click on the Hold button: which sends the points of your current score to the total one and your turn is over

Good luck and don't be too greedy
